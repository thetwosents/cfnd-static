A Team of Community Members Striving to Build a Better Dallas

The Coalition for a New Dallas’ mission is to rebuild the city’s urban core and revitalize neighborhoods by advocating for the advancement of public policies that improve mobility, increase opportunity and prosperity for all, and enhance the quality of life across Dallas.

Issues We're Committed To Solving

Transportation

The city of Dallas, once a powerful, sophisticated metropolis, began to lose its economic momentum to the suburbs in the 1970s, and the most visible cause for this decline was the erection of elevated interstate highways right through the city center.

Quality of Life

We must improve the quality of life in Dallas in order to transition from a commuter city to one that is livable and residential.

Read More

Housing

Dallas is 20,000 affordable housing units short. Where and how we choose to deploy our resources to shore this gap will determine whether we make any leeway in helping our poorest neighbors escape poverty.

Read More

Governance

Dallas must define its vision and a strategy. Then, city staff, council members, and appointees must be held accountable in implementing those principles.
