Our mission

The Coalition for a New Dallas is committed to making Dallas a walkable, diverse, and vibrant city. We are working to remove the physical and economic barriers that have divided our neighborhoods, re-enforced segregation, and stifled economic opportunity. Our short-term goal is to remake Dallas from a city built for commuters to a city built for its residents by reducing the primacy of the automobile, investing in multiple choices in mobility, and creating neighborhoods where people can live, work, and play. Our long-term goal is to restore whole neighborhoods of sufficient density throughout out city where jobs and basic services--housing, transit, schools, parks, and retail—are available within a short distance navigable by everyone.
